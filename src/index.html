Our Story

Founded on the shores of Rehoboth Beach, Coastal Belle brings the beauty of the ocean into your home through meticulously crafted shell art. Each piece is handcrafted with shells collected from our local beaches and around the world.

Our artisans blend traditional techniques with contemporary design to create unique pieces that celebrate coastal living. From delicate jewelry to stunning home décor, every creation embodies our passion for the sea.
